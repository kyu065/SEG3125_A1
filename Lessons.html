SWIMMING LESSONS

    Topic: Basic Swimming Techniques

    Objectives: Learn fundamental swimming strokes and breathing techniques

    Materials: Swimsuit, goggles, swim cap

    Procedure: Warm-up, stroke practice, breathing exercises, cool-down

    Assessment: Skill demonstration and feedback

POWER SWIM

    Topic: Advanced Swimming Techniques and Endurance

    Objectives: Enhance stroke efficiency and build endurance

    Materials: Swimsuit, goggles, swim cap, kickboard

    Procedure: Intense interval training, technique drills, endurance sets

    Assessment: Timed swims and performance tracking

WATER SAFETY

    Topic: Essential Water Safety Skills

    Objectives: Understand and practice water safety measures

    Materials: Swimsuit, life vest (provided), rescue equipment, boots

    Procedure: Safety talks, rescue techniques, safe swimming practices

    Assessment: Safety drills and scenario-based assessments

PRIVATE SWIM SESSIONS

    Topic: Customized Swim Training

    Objectives: Achieve personal swimming goals with private coaching for any number of swimmers

    Materials: Swimsuit, goggles, swim cap, fins, kickboard, handpaddles

    Procedure: Personalized lesson plan, targeted exercises, progress review

    Assessment: Continuous feedback and goal reassessment
